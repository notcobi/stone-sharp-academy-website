What to Expect

This session helps us identify your child's math gaps, strengths, learning style, and current skill level so we can build a personalized plan.

  • 30-45 minute online assessment with the student
  • Covers core skills, problem-solving, and course readiness
  • Follow-up call to review results and next steps

Common Questions

Is the assessment really free?
Yes. There's no cost and no obligation. It's just about finding out where your child is.

How long does it take?
30-45 minutes online with the student, plus a follow-up call to review results.

Will I get pricing information?
Yes. After the assessment, we provide a clear breakdown of pricing based on the recommended session format and frequency.

What if we're not ready to start right away?
No problem. The assessment gives you useful information no matter when or if you decide to start.
