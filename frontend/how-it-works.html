Our Tutoring

What Makes Our Tutoring Work

Three things that set our students up for real improvement.

1-on-1 Tutoring Sessions

Every session is built around your child's level, learning style, and a prepared plan.

  • Matched with a consistent tutor
  • 60 or 90 minute sessions
  • Online or in-person (we come to you)
  • Structured around a personalized plan

Custom Worksheets

After every session, your child gets worksheets targeting exactly what was covered, not generic practice.

  • Built for each student
  • Reinforces session learning
  • Targets weak areas and gaps
  • New worksheets after every session

Daily Drop-In Support

Students get stuck between sessions. Drop-ins let them get help the same day.

  • Available daily online
  • Homework, worksheets, or test prep help
  • Included with every program

Tutor Standards

  • Carefully matched to each student
  • Strong Grades 9-12 math background
  • Consistent teaching approach
  • Ongoing support from our team
