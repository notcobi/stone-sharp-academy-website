About Us

Meet the Founder

Torsten Sopczak

I've been surrounded by math my entire life. Engineering pushed me to live in it every day, but even then, I knew what it felt like to struggle without real guidance.

When I started tutoring, I saw the same thing everywhere: tutors without a plan, sessions that didn't go anywhere, and students stuck between meetings with no one to ask.

I knew there had to be a better way. So I built one.

At Stone Sharp Academy, every student gets:

  • A diagnostic assessment to find the real gaps
  • A personalized study plan built from real data
  • Targeted worksheets after every session
  • A tutor who shows up prepared, not just filling time
  • Same-day help between sessions through daily drop-ins

This isn't a one-tutor service. It's a team-run program focused on one thing: Grades 9–12 math.

Our Philosophy

We believe students don't struggle because they aren't good at math.

Most students struggle because:

  • Gaps build up over time
  • Sessions aren't structured
  • There's no support between lessons

That's why everything we do is designed to be:

  • Clear and structured
  • Alberta curriculum aligned
  • Focused on real understanding, not just homework help

When students understand the foundations, confidence and grades improve naturally.
